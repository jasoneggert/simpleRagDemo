# Billing Support Handbook

## Refund windows

Monthly subscriptions are refundable within 14 calendar days of the most recent charge if the
workspace used fewer than 25 API calls after renewal. Annual subscriptions are refundable within
30 calendar days if the account has not exported data or invited more than 3 users during that
window.

## Proration rules

Upgrades take effect immediately and create a prorated invoice for the remainder of the billing
period. Downgrades do not create refunds. Downgrades schedule the lower plan for the next renewal.

## Duplicate charges

If two successful charges for the same invoice amount appear within 24 hours, treat the second
charge as a potential duplicate. Support may issue a full refund for the duplicate charge after
confirming the customer did not intentionally purchase a second workspace.

## Courtesy credits

Support can offer a courtesy credit of up to 20 percent of the last invoice when the platform had
confirmed availability issues for more than 4 hours in a single billing cycle. Courtesy credits do
not apply to taxes or prepaid annual contracts.
