# Tax and Invoice Notes

## VAT and GST

Customers with a valid tax ID on file before renewal can receive reverse-charge treatment where
supported. Support should not manually refund tax after an invoice is finalized unless finance
operations confirms the tax status was collected incorrectly.

## Invoice edits

Issued invoices are immutable. Support may update billing contact details for future invoices, but
cannot change the legal entity name or tax amount on a finalized invoice without finance approval.

## Receipt requests

Customers can download receipts from the billing portal after a successful payment. If they cannot
access the portal, support may resend the invoice receipt email to the billing contact on file.

## Refund exclusions

Taxes, currency conversion differences, and bank fees are not refundable as courtesy adjustments.
